Some useful tips from Joana and Isabel to those aiming to attend in person:

Upon arrival at the airport Francisco de Sá Carneiro, Porto, there are several ways to get to Porto city centre:

  • By METRO – there is a station at the airport (Violet line (E)). You need to get a card (“Andante azul” 0.60€) and charge it with a Z4 trip (2.00€). This can be made in the automatic machines located in every Metro station. With this card you can get to the station Casa da Música (connection with several buses) or Trindade (city centre – Av. Aliados). To get to the Faculty of Pharmacy University of Porto (FFUP), go to Casa da Música station, where you can get several connection buses, such as 507 or 208 that stop at FFUP (Palácio de Cristal). Click for the map of the Metro line. To plan your trip, you can go to en.metrodoporto.pt/. IMPORTANT: before entering the metro, make sure to validate the card. The title is valid for 1 h trip. By Metro/bus the trip from airport to FFUP will take around 40 minutes.
  • By BUS – there is also an option to go to the city centre by bus 601, which goes from the airport to Casa da Música that takes about 50 minutes. For other options, consult this page
  • By TAXI – there are taxis in the arrival zone at the airport. The trip to the city centre costs around 25-30€ and takes about 20-30 minutes.
  • By TRANSFER – there are several options for transfers. The trip to the city centre costs around 25-30€ and takes about 20-30 minutes. Example via this link
  • By UBER – the platform works quite well in Porto city. The trip from the airport to the city centre costs about 10-20€ and takes about 20-30 minutes.
